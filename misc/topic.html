Facial Recognition

Facial recognition is a technology that is capable of matching up human faces including digital image or video against facial recognition database, which this technology can help legal operation department to locate and secure suspect. Face recognition technology is based on the facial features of a person. For the input face image or video stream, it first determines whether there is a face. If there is a face, then the position, size and main points of each face are further given. Location information of facial organs. And based on this information, further extract the identity features contained in each face, and compare them with known faces, so as to identify the identity of each face. The broad sense of face recognition actually includes a series of related technologies for building a face recognition system, including face image collection, face positioning, face recognition preprocessing, identity confirmation, and identity search, etc.; while the narrow sense of face recognition specifically refers to the adoption of A technology or system for confirming or finding the identity of a person's face.

With the rapid increase in society's reliance on technology, Facial Recognition is an advancement in the security of personal devices, as well as the safety of individuals.
