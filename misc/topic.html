Facial Recognition

facial recognition is a technology that is capable of matching up human faces including digital image or video against facial recognition database, which this technology can help legal operation department to locate and secure suspect.

With the rapid increase in society's reliance on technology, Facial Recognition is an advancement in the security of personal devices, as well as the safety of individuals.
