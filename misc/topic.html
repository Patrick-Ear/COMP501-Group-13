Facial Recognition

Facial recognition is a technology that is capable of matching a persons biometric data including digital image or video with from a database of other biometric data, which this technology we are able to apply the results from the facial recognition to our lives, from using the facial recognition systems to aid in the capturing of criminals to adding convinience to our everday lives such as shopping or authentication services.

With the rapid increase in society's reliance on technology, Facial Recognition is an important advancement in the security of personal property, as well as the safety and convinience of individuals and thier everyday lives.
